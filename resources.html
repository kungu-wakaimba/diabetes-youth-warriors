General Diabetes Information

  • What is Diabetes?
    A chronic health condition that affects how your body turns food into energy, mainly due to issues with insulin.
  • Types:
    • Type 1: Autoimmune, usually diagnosed in children or young adults.
    • Type 2: More common, often related to lifestyle and genetics.
    • Gestational: Occurs during pregnancy.
  • Symptoms: Frequent urination, increased thirst, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, blurred vision.
  • Management: Healthy eating, regular physical activity, medication/insulin, regular check-ups, stress management.

Diet & Nutrition Tips

  • Choose whole grains, fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Be mindful of portion sizes and carbohydrate counting.
  • Limit added sugars, sugary drinks, and processed foods.
  • Eat regular meals and snacks to maintain blood sugar stability.
  • Stay hydrated with water or unsweetened drinks.
  • Explore EatRight: Diabetes Nutrition
